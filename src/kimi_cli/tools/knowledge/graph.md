# Knowledge Graph Discovery Tools

These tools allow you to explore the connections between documents in the Knowledge Base.

## WikiBacklinks

Find all documents that link TO a specific document. This helps you understand the context and "upstream" dependencies of a piece of information.

**When to use:**
- When you want to see what other notes reference the current one.
- To find broader topics that encompass a specific detail.

## WikiRelated

Find documents that are semantically or structurally related to a target document.

**Relations include:**
- Direct links (both inbound and outbound).
- Shared tags (at least 2 tags in common).

Results are ranked by the strength of the connection (direct links are weighted higher than tag matches).

**When to use:**
- To discover similar content that might not be explicitly linked.
- To explore a topic cluster.
